ABOUT ISTE

We are a Society, We are a Family, We are an Organization

The Indian Society for Technical Education is a non-profit organisation that was created in 1968 at IIT Delhi with the goal of building links between students and instructors from different universities and evaluating their talents and abilities. It is a student-led national executive council with over 5 lakh members from over 2345 technical education institutes, including NITs, IITs, and other noteworthy institutions.
